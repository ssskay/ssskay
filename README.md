## ⭐ Stuff I've built

The ones for other people to actually use.

### 🤖 Agentic tools — you talk, they work

Each one ships with a Claude Code skill, and each one has a line the AI can't cross. That's the feature.

#### 🏛️ [internet-historian](https://github.com/ssskay/internet-historian)

*Web pages die. Yours don't have to.*

A patient Wayback Machine archiver for macOS that treats a busy Internet Archive as weather, not failure — it retries forever until your pages are preserved. Say *"archive Chiikawa stuff"* and it handles the rest. `pipx install internet-historian`.

#### 📖 [wiki-gap-finder](https://github.com/ssskay/wiki-gap-finder)

*The AI does the detective work; a human writes every sentence.*

Finds people from underrepresented groups who are missing from English Wikipedia, then fact-checks every claim into a research dossier a human writes the article from. Zero API keys.

#### 📦 [inbox-catalog](https://github.com/ssskay/inbox-catalog)

*Context for your agents, straight from your own inbox.*

Local-first, read-only engine that turns your order emails into structured data your agents can actually use: sort purchases by project, track spending in categories that match how *you* think, stop re-buying things you already own. Installs as a Claude Code plugin with a zero-setup demo.

### 🖥️→🍎 Desktop pet rescue

New hobby unlocked: adopting cute Windows-only desktop apps and giving them a second life on macOS. To be clear — these aren't my creations. The original developers did the hard, lovely work; I do the porting, the Mac-specific bug hunts, and send fixes back upstream where I can.

#### 🐰 [Yaha-Pet for macOS](https://github.com/ssskay/Yaha-Pet)

*Ura! Yaha!*

Port of [gitChara-dot's Yaha-Pet](https://github.com/gitChara-dot/Yaha-Pet) (MIT): Usagi, Hachiware & Chiikawa roam your desktop, squeak when grabbed, and now live in your Dock. Fixed a crash and an animation bug along the way — [offered upstream](https://github.com/gitChara-dot/Yaha-Pet/pull/2).

#### 🍙 [Desktop Chiikawa for macOS](https://github.com/ssskay/desktop-chiikawa-macos)

*Drink water. Chiikawa is watching.*

Port of [CookieElmo's Desktop Chiikawa](https://cookieelmo.itch.io/desktop-chiikawa), the wellness-reminder pet from itch.io. Rebuilt on the native Godot runtime with some new tricks: wandering, petting, snacks, kaomoji speech, and it remembers where you left it.
